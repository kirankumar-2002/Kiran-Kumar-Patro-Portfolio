About Me

I am an Associate Software Engineer at Accenture with over a year of hands-on experience in enterprise application development and healthcare technology solutions. With a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and analytical problem-solving skills to every project.

Currently working on the Mt Sinai AMS project at Accenture, I specialize in ServiceNow, Oracle SQL, Oracle PL/SQL, and Oracle APEX development. My role involves designing and implementing robust database solutions and enterprise applications that streamline healthcare operations and enhance system efficiency.

My technical foundation includes Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, and modern web development practices, complemented by certifications in Oracle PL/SQL, Oracle APEX, and EMR systems. I am passionate about emerging technologies and currently focusing on Agentic AI to stay at the forefront of innovation in software development.

My academic projects, including "Gas Leakage Detection" using Arduino and MQ2 Gas Sensor, and "Third Eye of the Blind" for visually impaired assistance, demonstrate my commitment to creating technology that makes a real-world impact.

Beyond my technical skills, I excel in analytical problem-solving, adaptability, and collaborative teamwork. I am proficient in Telugu, Odia, Hindi, and English, enabling effective communication in diverse professional environments. As a former NSS member and active volunteer, I remain committed to giving back to the community.
